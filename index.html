The Attic at Waterside is a restaurant that takes you back to the prohibition era with its vintage decor and menu. The restaurant specializes in serving delicious food, including burgers that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. The ambiance of the restaurant is cozy, intimate, and nostalgic, which makes it the perfect spot to relax and enjoy a meal with friends or family. Whether you are a fan of the prohibition era or just looking for a great dining experience, The Attic at Waterside is definitely worth a visit.
